My Dearest Pragati,

The best things really do happen when you stop looking for them. That's how it was with you. When we matched online, I almost didn't message you. After everything that happened before, my heart just wasn't ready to be disappointed again. I waited until there were only two hours left on that match. Two hours that changed everything. Then we matched. Like the universe was saying, "Try again. This one's different." It was right.

I keep all these little memories of you close to my heart. The way you push your hair behind your ears, how your cheeks look in the soft light. I remember seeing you under the warm lights at the CCD cafÃ©, looking so peaceful and beautiful. I've wanted to kiss your cheek so many times. But I wanted to take things slow, to really build something real with you. With you, everything feels special. Remember that boat ride? I had this whole quiet, romantic plan to hold your hand and just talk. But instead we ended up laughing and talking about a hundred different things, getting completely off track. And it was better than anything I could have planned. That's what I love about us.

I love our long talks. I love how you tell me everything, the big stuff and the little stuff. And I love how you remember things I've told you, even small things. It makes me feel like you're really listening, like I really matter to you. Walking with you by the lake, holding your hand, that's when I feel most at peace. It's not the water or the trees, it's you beside me. Your hand in mine. I also think about that day at the fair. When everything got crazy and stressful, you stayed so calm. You didn't get upset or flustered. You were just steady. I saw how strong you are in that moment. And I'm always amazed by how you always say yes when I ask to see you. You're always ready, always there. That means the world to me.

We're each other's first real love (or mine atleast), and that feels so special. I'm so glad we've been able to be close in that way too. Those moments brought us to a different kind of understanding. They made me feel connected to you on a level that's hard to put into words. I feel for you, completely and truly.

We're figuring this out together, and that's the beautiful part. I don't just want this to be a beautiful memory. I want to build a real, lasting love with you. I know we can. I promise to always listen, to always care about the big days and the quiet ones, and to always walk beside you, holding your hand. Thank you for being you. Thank you for choosing me.

With all my love,
